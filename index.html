About Us

Marseh sa Kerr (MSK) makes life easier for women by delivering fresh vegetables (ndugar) to their doorsteps with convenience and quality. We also process and package traditional African foods, serving both the local market and the diaspora who long for authentic Gambian flavors.

Our mission is to provide convenient access to fresh, high-quality vegetables and traditional foods while supporting local farmers and preserving our culinary heritage.
